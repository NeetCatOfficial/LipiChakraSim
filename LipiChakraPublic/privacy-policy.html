Privacy Policy — Lipi Chakra

Homepage

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Lipi Chakra is a custom Devanagari (Hindi) keyboard for Android. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

Summary

Lipi Chakra does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any of your data. The app has no internet permission and cannot send anything off your device.

What the app stores, and where

Lipi Chakra learns, per character, how you personally tend to drag when you mean that character, so it can nudge ambiguous gestures toward your own habits over time. This includes:

  • Per-character gesture statistics (release angle, distance, and timing patterns)
  • Aggregate counts of commits and corrections, used to show your typing accuracy on the in-app Stats screen

This data is stored only in the app's private local storage on your device (Android SharedPreferences). It never leaves your device — it is not uploaded to any server, not shared with any third party, and the app has no network access with which to do so.

What the app does not do

  • It does not log, store, or transmit the words or sentences you type through the keyboard.
  • It does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • It does not request microphone, contacts, location, or storage permissions.

Your control over stored data

The in-app Stats screen lets you reset the learned data for a single character, or clear all learned data at once, at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

Lipi Chakra does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
