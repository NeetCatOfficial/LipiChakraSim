How the gesture works (tap to expand)
  • Short drag toward a direction previews/commits that group's first letter.
  • Drag past ~42% of the radius and it locks onto that group — the rest fan out clockwise starting exactly where you dragged to.
  • Vowels use the exact same mechanic and spacing as consonants now — lock in, then sweep clockwise for the next form.
  • अ after a consonant inserts halant (्) instead of nothing — that's how conjuncts get built.
  • Return all the way to the center to cancel a drag.
  • ⌨️ My keyboard mode: practice with your actual installed keyboard instead of the on-screen pads — good for building real muscle memory once the basics feel familiar.
